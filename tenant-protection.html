✓ What's Covered

  • ✓ Fire, lightning, explosion, smoke
  • ✓ Windstorm, hail, tornado, hurricane
  • ✓ Water damage (burst pipes / appliances)
  • ✓ Rodent damage
  • ✓ Vandalism & malicious mischief
  • ✓ Burglary (forced entry with visible marks)
  • ✓ Earthquake, sinkhole collapse
  • ✓ Flood (up to 50% of coverage limit / $2,000 max)
  • ✓ Transit within 100 miles (fire, collision)
  • ✓ Debris removal (25% of claim amount)

✗ What's NOT Covered

  • ✗ Jewelry, watches, precious stones, furs
  • ✗ Money, currency, securities, documents
  • ✗ Firearms, gold / silver / platinum
  • ✗ Theft or unexplained disappearance
  • ✗ Wear and tear, gradual deterioration
  • ✗ Insects, vermin (not rodent damage)
  • ✗ Mechanical breakdown
  • ✗ Sewer backup flood

This is a partial list. See the full Certificate of Property Insurance for all exclusions and conditions.
